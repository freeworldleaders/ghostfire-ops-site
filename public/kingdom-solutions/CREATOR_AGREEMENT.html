# Creator Agreement (Plain Language, Non-Predatory)

**Effective date:** TBD

## 1) You own your content
Everything you upload or create remains yours.

## 2) License to operate the service
You grant us a limited license to host, process, and display your content **only** as needed to provide the service you choose (private, unlisted, or public).

## 3) No training on private content
We do **not** train AI models on private content.  
If we ever introduce optional training programs, they will be separate and opt-in with clear terms.

## 4) Publishing and monetization are opt-in
Nothing is published or monetized without your explicit action.

## 5) Revenue share (if monetization enabled)
If you opt into monetization, revenue splits and payout schedules will be shown clearly before activation.
- No exclusivity
- No perpetual rights
- You can turn monetization off

## 6) Leaving the platform
You can export your work and delete your account at any time.

## 7) Safety and integrity
You agree not to upload content you don’t have rights to or that violates Trust & Safety.

## 8) Changes
If we change this agreement, we will notify you and provide a clear summary.
