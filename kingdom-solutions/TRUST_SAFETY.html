# Trust & Safety (Plain Language)

## Our goal
Keep creators safe. Protect privacy. Prevent exploitation.

## What you can post
- Your own stories, recordings, and experiences
- Content you have permission to share
- Educational and documentary material

## What is not allowed
- Impersonation or identity fraud
- Non-consensual recordings or private data leaks
- Harassment, threats, or targeted hate
- Sexual content involving minors (zero tolerance)
- Instructions to commit violence or wrongdoing
- Content that exploits vulnerable people

## Privacy defaults
- New stories are **private by default**
- Sharing requires an explicit action each time
- You control visibility: **Private / Unlisted / Public**

## Reporting
You can report content for:
- harassment
- impersonation
- privacy violations
- copyright issues
- safety concerns

## What happens after a report
1) We review
2) We may restrict visibility during review
3) We remove content that violates policy
4) We may suspend accounts for repeated or severe violations

## Appeals
If you think we made a mistake, you can appeal and we will review again.

## Data deletion
You can request deletion of your content and account. We will honor deletion requests except where law requires retention.
